# 🏥 Pharmacy Deep Sales & Customer Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI project provides a 360-degree view of a retail pharmacy's performance. It transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights, focusing on two main pillars: **Sales Growth Dynamics** and **Customer Behavior Analysis (RFM)**.

## 🚀 Key Features & Dashboards

### 1. Sales Performance & YoY Analysis
*   **Revenue Tracking:** Detailed monitoring of annual sales (e.g., 7.27M SAR) with a 13% YoY growth rate.
*   **Product Performance:** Identification of Top/Bottom products using advanced filtering (e.g., focusing on items with sales > 5000).
*   **Trend Analysis:** Monthly sales comparisons to identify seasonal peaks and dips.

### 2. Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)
*   **Strategic Categorization:** Segmenting customers into **Champions, Loyal, At Risk, and Others** based on Recency, Frequency, and Monetary values.
*   **Scatter Plot Visualization:** A deep dive into customer distribution to help the marketing team target "At Risk" high-value customers.
*   **Loyalty Insights:** Identifying top contributors to revenue by segment.

### 3. Growth & Decline Analytics
*   **Positive Growth:** Highlighting products with exceptional growth (up to 2922%) to optimize inventory.
*   **Negative Growth:** Identifying "bleeding" products (100% decline) to prevent future losses.
*   **Project-Based Filtering:** Ability to drill down into specific sales channels (CallCenter, eCommerce, Insurance, etc.).

## 🛠️ Technical Skills Demonstrated
*   **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Created complex measures for YoY Growth %, RFM Scoring, and Dynamic Filtering.
*   **Power Query (M):** Extensive data cleaning, merging sales data with project metadata, and data transformation.
*   **Data Modeling:** Implementation of a Star Schema for optimized performance.
*   **Data Masking:** Applied professional anonymization to sensitive product and customer names to ensure data privacy.
*   **UI/UX Design:** Built an intuitive, user-friendly interface with consistent branding and navigation buttons.

## 📊 How to Use
1. Download the `.pbix` file.
2. Open with Power BI Desktop.
3. Use the **Navigation Buttons** at the top to switch between "Sales Analysis", "Growth Analysis", and "Customer Segmentation".
4. Use the **Reset** button to clear all filters instantly.
